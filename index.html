Mark Fox

Crafting Original Scores for the Dark, the Disturbing, and the Divine

Biography

Mark Fox is a UK-based film composer known for crafting emotionally resonant and sonically rich scores that elevate storytelling across genres. With a rare ability to blend orchestral depth, electronic sophistication, and experimental edge, Mark brings a unique, cinematic voice to every project.

Trained at Dartington under avant-garde pioneers Edward Cowie and Frank Denyer, he has a deep understanding of contemporary orchestral techniques, which he fuses seamlessly with modern and global traditions. His score for The Texas Chainsaw Massacre: The Shocking Truth established his reputation for immersive, boundary-pushing soundscapes.

Mark personally performs all instrumental parts—ranging from bass, guitar, synths and percussion—ensuring cohesive, expressive results. He designs his own instruments and custom effects (reverbs, oscillators, microphones), crafted to meet each project’s emotional demands.

Whether scoring psychological thrillers, documentaries or cult horror, Mark delivers bold, original work that directors trust. Formerly a member of Shamus Oblivion and the Megadeath Morris Men until 2015, he is now the in-house composer for Redclaw Media and recently supported Public Image Ltd live at Rock City with the band Stuart Pearce.
