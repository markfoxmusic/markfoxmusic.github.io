About Mark

Mark Fox is a UK-based composer best known for his work in horror and underground cinema. His music has featured in films such as Wasterville, Skullcracker, Death Molester: Barn of Blood, and The Shocking Truth. His soundtrack work includes releases with Scare Flair Records and theatrical screenings via Grindhouse Releasing. Mark’s work has been mentioned in media such as Desperate Living and Daily Dead.

He is currently working on new projects including Amityville Trailer Park and Death Receipt.
