About Me

I am Mark Fox, a dedicated film composer specializing in creating atmospheric and gripping scores for horror and indie films. My work includes the chilling soundtracks for films such as Wasterville, Death Molester Barn of Blood, and ongoing projects like Skullcracker, Amityville Trailer Park, and Death Receipt.

With a deep understanding of mood, tension, and storytelling through music, I aim to elevate every film project I contribute to. My compositions have been praised for their immersive qualities and unique blend of traditional and experimental elements.

Film Scores

  • Wasterville — Atmospheric horror score enhancing the eerie mood and tension.
  • Death Molester Barn of Blood — A dark, unsettling soundtrack matching the film’s raw intensity.
  • Skullcracker — Currently in production, promising a mix of aggressive and haunting themes.
  • Amityville Trailer Park — A fresh take on horror scoring with experimental sound design.
  • Death Receipt — Upcoming project focusing on minimalist suspense music.
