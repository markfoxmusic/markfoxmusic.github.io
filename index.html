Biography

Mark Fox is a UK-based film composer known for crafting emotionally resonant and sonically rich scores that elevate storytelling across genres. With a rare ability to blend orchestral depth, electronic sophistication, and experimental edge, Mark brings a unique, cinematic voice to every project he works on.

Trained at Dartington under avant-garde pioneers Edward Cowie and Frank Denyer, Mark has a deep-rooted understanding of contemporary orchestral techniques, which he seamlessly fuses with modern and global musical traditions. His score for The Texas Chainsaw Massacre: The Shocking Truth established his reputation for creating immersive, boundary-pushing soundscapes—yet his creative range extends far beyond the experimental.

A skilled multi-instrumentalist, he personally performs all instrumental parts—ranging from bass, guitar, and synths to keyboards and custom textures—ensuring a cohesive and nuanced sonic identity. His hands-on, artisanal approach includes designing and building his own instruments and custom effects units.

Whether scoring psychological thrillers, art-house films, documentaries, or genre cinema, Mark Fox delivers bold, original music that directors and producers trust to bring their vision to life.
